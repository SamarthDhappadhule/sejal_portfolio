About Me

Hello , I'm Sejal ...

A G.D. Art graduate, photographer, cinematographer, and visual storyteller. My creative journey began in childhood, where I found peace in colors and brushstrokes. What started as painting soon evolved into capturing life through the lensturning moments, emotions, and stories into timeless visuals.

Over the last six years, I’ve worked professionally in portrait photography, travel stories, cinematography, and social media content creation. With my fine arts background, I bring an artistic eye to every frame balancing light, composition, and emotion to create visuals that truly connect.

I believe photography and film are not just about capturing what you see, but about expressing what you feel. Every portrait, every travel frame, every cinematic moment carries a piece of the story behind it—and my mission is to tell those stories in the most authentic and impactful way.
